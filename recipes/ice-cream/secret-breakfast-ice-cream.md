Secret Breakfast ice cream
==

Ingredients
--

* 2 c `heavy cream`
* 1 c `whole milk`
* 2 t `salt`
* 3 `egg yolks`
* 1 c `sugar`
* 1/2 t `vanilla extract`
* 1/2 c `bourbon`
* 1/2 c [`corn flake cookies`](../cookies/corn-flake-cookies.md)*chopped*

Directions
--

Notes
--

* I usually only use about 1 t of salt and even then I just sort of eyeball it.

Fine print

### Yield

1 qt
